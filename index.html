The Practice

What is Chen Tai Chi?

Chen-style Tai Chi Chuan is the oldest and original form of Tai Chi, developed in Chen Village, Henan Province, China over 400 years ago. It is characterized by its distinctive combination of slow, flowing movements and explosive bursts of power.

Unlike other Tai Chi styles, Chen style retains its martial arts applications while offering profound health benefits — improved balance, reduced stress, enhanced flexibility, and a calm, focused mind.

Whether you are seeking healing, fitness, self-defense, or spiritual growth, Chen Tai Chi offers a lifetime of discovery.
